Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for Clean and Squeak Services, Inc.

Welcome to Clean and Squeak Services, Inc. ("Clean and Squeak," "we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share personal information when you use our website or services.

1. What We Collect

  • Identity data: Name, phone number, email, address
  • Account data: Login credentials, booking preferences
  • Service data: Orders, ratings, and messages
  • Payment data: Processed via third-party systems
  • Device data: IP address, device type, browser version
  • Location data: Only with your permission

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To match customers with service providers
  • To process bookings and payments
  • To send confirmations, alerts, and feedback requests
  • To improve features and user experience
  • To meet legal and regulatory obligations

3. Who We Share With

  • Cleaners and handymen assigned to your jobs
  • Cloud services, customer support, and analytics tools
  • Regulators or authorities when legally required

4. Cookies

We use cookies for login, session management, and analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Security

Your data is stored using encryption and secured protocols. No online service is 100% secure, but we follow industry standards.

6. Your Rights

  • Update or delete your information by logging in
  • Unsubscribe from promotional messages anytime
  • Request data deletion by emailing support@cleanandsqueak.com

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are for adults only. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 18.

8. Policy Updates

This policy may change. Updates will be posted here with an effective date. Continued use means you accept the changes.

9. Contact

Have questions? Contact us at support@cleanandsqueak.com.
